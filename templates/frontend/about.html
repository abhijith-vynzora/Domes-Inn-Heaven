Our History

The Domes Inn Heaven Story

Domes Inn Heaven was born from a simple question: What if luxury had no walls? We've grown from a single hillside dome into Kerala's premier glamping retreat, designed to connect you deeply with the nature around you.

Redefining Nature Luxury

To be the world’s most sought-after glamping destination where luxury and wilderness coexist in perfect harmony, inspiring a deeper connection with the Earth.

Sustainable & Soulful Stays

To provide exceptional hospitality through eco-friendly dome stays, ensuring every guest experiences the raw beauty of Kerala without compromising on premium comfort.

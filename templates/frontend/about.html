Our History

The Domes Inn Heaven Story

It started with a hillside, an open sky, and a question: What if the most luxurious hotel room had no walls?

Domes Inn Heaven was born from that question — and from a deep, personal love for Kerala's extraordinary natural beauty. Our founders, avid travellers themselves, noticed that the most powerful travel memories were never about thread counts or city skylines. They were about waking up to mist rolling over a valley, or sitting still as a forest came alive at dusk. They wanted to build a place that made those moments the entire point of the stay.

The first dome went up on a quiet hillside in Kerala, surrounded by the kind of scenery that makes you forget your phone exists. Guests arrived expecting a novelty. They left describing it as a life experience.

Since then, Domes Inn Heaven has grown — thoughtfully and intentionally — into a full glamping retreat with multiple uniquely designed domes, each one a distinct experience in itself. From the romance of the Stargazer Dome to the cultural richness of the Heritage Dome, every structure is designed to connect you more deeply with the place you're in.

Our Values

We believe that luxury is not about excess — it is about attention. Attention to the quality of your sleep, the flavour of your food, the view from your pillow, and the feeling you carry home. We are committed to sustainable practices that protect the very landscape that makes us what we are, from eco-friendly construction to locally sourced meals.

We are also deeply proud of our team — a close-knit family of guides, hosts, chefs, and caretakers who treat every guest as a personal guest of the house.

Domes Inn Heaven is not just a place to stay. It is a place to remember.

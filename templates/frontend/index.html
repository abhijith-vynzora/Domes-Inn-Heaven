The Story Behind Domes Inn Heaven

Domes Inn Heaven was founded with a single, bold idea — that the most extraordinary hotel room is one built by nature itself. Starting with just a handful of geodesic domes set against Kerala's breathtaking hills, we have grown into a retreat that welcomes travellers from across India and beyond. Every dome we build is a tribute to the land it stands on.

Our mission is to offer guests a stay that is deeply connected to nature, without sacrificing comfort or style. We believe that luxury and sustainability can coexist, and every decision we make — from the materials we use to the meals we serve — reflects that belief. We exist to remind our guests that the earth, at its finest, is the ultimate luxury.

We envision a future where travellers choose slow, meaningful escapes over rushed tourism. Domes Inn Heaven aspires to be Kerala's most celebrated glamping destination — a place where every guest leaves not just rested, but genuinely transformed by the beauty of what surrounds them.
